Indicator 2 – Population Growth Rate

This is a measure of the rate of population growth experienced in the region as a whole, and the proportion of the growth that is occurring in the Priority Growth Areas (PGAs), Cluster Country Residential Areas (CCRAs), and in other areas. It measures the growth rate over the five-year period between Census years.

Why is this Indicator Important?

Tracking growth rates helps interpret the pace of growth in the region. The growth rate informs the timeframe requirements for implementing new initiatives, such as new housing and transit.
